一些细碎念

无穷的远方，无尽的人们，其实，与我也没有什么关系。

或许你已经注意到，大人，或者说你的父母，其实没什么朋友。或许你已经注意到，你未来似乎仅仅和亲戚，旁边因为各种因素而产生联系的人会有一点交集。你遇见过许多人，然后这些人就再也没出现过。你也没遇见过什么人，他们连出现都没有过。

进入大学，每天都能碰到各种各样的人，然后，忘记，再遇见，忘记。偶尔有那么几个外表，不经意间吸引了你，产生了些许记忆，然后是一些臆想与虚构，但很多仅此而已。我们因班级，社团什么的产生交集，我们因某些意外产生联系……这些就被我们称作：缘分。

那少的可怜的缘分！更多时候，要靠我们自己主动，所谓搭讪。但不幸的社会，竞争残酷，人性之下，确实难以信任。唯有相互坦诚，才能找到让你感觉有关系的那位。否则，不过也会慢慢消失罢了。我们也要感谢血缘（基因），给我们带来了与生俱来的关系。否则，我们一开始就只是孤独一人。

所谓茕茕孑立，踽踽独行。

Some small thoughts

Endless distance, endless people, in fact, have nothing to do with me.

As you may have noticed, adults, or your parents, don’t really have many friends. You may have noticed that your future only seems to have a little overlap with relatives and people around you who are connected for various reasons. You meet a lot of people, and they’re never seen again. You never met anyone. They never even showed up.

Enter the university, can meet all kinds of people every day, and then, forget, meet again, forget. Occasionally there are a few appearances, inadvertently attracted to you, produced a few memories, and then some imagination and fiction, but a lot of that is nothing more. We crossed paths in classes, clubs, whatever, we connected by accident…… These are what we call: fate.

That little poor fate! More often, we have to rely on our own initiative, the so-called pick-up. But the unfortunate society, the competition is cruel, human nature, it is difficult to trust. The only way to find someone who makes you feel connected is to be honest with each other. Otherwise, it will slowly disappear. We also have blood (genes) to thank for the relationships we are born with. Otherwise, we’re all alone in the first place.

All alone, walking alone.

(英文来自直接有道翻译)

留下万分之一点，采得孤人所笑言
